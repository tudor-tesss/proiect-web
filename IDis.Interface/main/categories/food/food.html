Quesadilla is a Mexican dish that consists of a tortilla folded in half and filled with cheese and other ingredients. It is then grilled or baked until the cheese melts and the tortilla is crisp. The tortilla is usually made of corn, but flour tortillas are also used. The dish is popular in Mexico and in the United States, where it is often served as a snack or appetizer.

A cheeseburger is a hamburger topped with cheese. Traditionally, the slice of cheese is placed on top of the meat patty, but the burger can include variations in structure, ingredients, and composition. In the United States, the cheese is often sliced from a larger block of cheese, such as American cheese, Cheddar cheese, or Swiss cheese. In other countries, such as the United Kingdom, the cheese is often grated.
