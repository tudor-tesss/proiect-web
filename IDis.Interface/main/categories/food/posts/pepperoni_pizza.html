Pepperoni pizza is a pizza with pepperoni as a topping. It is a popular pizza in the United States, Canada, and Australia. Pepperoni is a type of salami made from cured pork and beef. It is usually sliced into thin rounds and added to pizza. Pepperoni is also used as a topping for other foods, such as sandwiches, pasta, and salads.
